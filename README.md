# 👋 Hi, I'm Pleniluner
• Greetings to friends from all corners of the world!

• Pax omnibus. (Peace to all.)


## About Me
• 🏗️ Architect: Architecture is my core profession, focused on conceptual design, spatial creation, and bringing visionary structures to life.

• 📐 BIM (Building Information Modeling) Professional: I specialize in Building Information Modeling, utilizing it for detailed documentation, coordination, and data-driven management throughout the project lifecycle.

• 🎮 Strategy & Simulation Connoisseur: A dedicated player of grand strategy and simulation titles, with Sid Meier's Civilization VI being my foremost favorite, followed by Stellaris, Hearts of Iron IV, Victoria 3, Europa Universalis IV, Cities: Skylines, and Crusader Kings III. Beyond the digital realm, I also find deep enjoyment in classic board games such as Chinese Chess (Xiangqi), Japanese Shogi, and International Chess.

• 📡 Radio Amateur & Signal Chaser (Callsign: BG7LBJ): Licensed Class A, currently navigating the VHF/UHF bands with a hacked Quansheng UV-K5. My ears are in the static, listening for structure—while I build the DSP and SDR fluency needed for the HF privileges ahead.

• 🌌 Stargazer: Observing the night sky and unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos is a profound source of inspiration.

• 💻 Computer Science Explorer: I love solving problems with code and diving into new technologies.

• 📱 Android Developer: Passionate about creating intuitive and high-performing mobile applications.

• 🎵 Critical Listener: I chase the vanishing gap between a recording and a live performance. My listening space is a constant work-in-progress of room correction, DAC synergy, and the relentless pursuit of the 'black background'.

• 🎸 Acoustic Guitar Student: Learning to translate pressure into pitch on a Yamaha FG800M. Drawn to the warm, woody resonance of a dreadnought body—where six strings and a soundboard become the simplest kind of architecture.

• 🖌️ Ink Brush & Built Form: Trained in architectural sketching and sensitive to color's weight. Chinese ink wash painting (水墨画) is more than an interest—it's a design compass. I explore how its restraint, its dialogue between void and mass, can inform a contemporary architecture where classical silence and modern clarity coexist.

• 🎴 Gensokyo Denizen: Immersed in the universe of the *Touhou Project*—its music, its manga, its animated daydreams, and the legendary bullet-hell ballet. It's a world where ZUN's melodies and fan‑driven storytelling remind me that difficulty can be a form of grace, and that the richest universes are built note by note, page by page.
